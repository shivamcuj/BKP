Unit 1: Introduction CS & IT Dept

What is AI? (Definition & Aspects)

Standard Definition

AI computer science ki wo branch hai jiske through hum intelligent machines bana sakte hain. Ye machines:

  • Insaan ki tarah behave karti hain.
  • Humans ki tarah sochti (think) hain.
  • Autonomous decisions lene ki kshamta rakhti hain.

Core Key Aspects

1. Simulation

Human intelligence aur cognitive functions ko mimic (copy) karna.

2. Problem Solving

Complex problems ko intelligence ke saath solve karna.

3. Learning & Decision Making

Data se seekhna (improve performance) aur rules ke basis pe decisions lena.

Classification of AI

Type-I: Based on Capabilities

Narrow / Weak AI

Jo sirf ek dedicated task kar sake.

Examples: Facial recognition, playing chess, Netflix recommendations.

General AI

Jo insaan ki tarah koi bhi intellectual task efficiently kar sake.

Super AI

Wo level jahan machines Human Intelligence ko surpass kar dengi.

Type-II: Based on Functionality

1. Reactive Machines

Inke paas koi memory nahi hoti. Ye sirf present situation par respond karti hain.

2. Limited Memory

Past data use karke decisions leti hain. Eg: Self-driving cars.

3. Theory of Mind

Hypothetical. Insaani emotions aur beliefs samajhne wala AI.

4. Self Awareness

Hypothetical. Jiske paas apni consciousness aur goals honge.

Real-World Applications

Healthcare

MRI/CT scans analysis, drug discovery, and personalized medicine.

Finance

Fraud detection patterns, algorithmic trading, risk assessment.

Education

Intelligent tutoring, personalized learning, automated grading.

Customer Care

Chatbots using NLP, voice assistants, recommendations.

Exam Cheat Sheet

Key Definitions

  • AI Objective: Mimic human cognitive functions like learning and problem-solving.
  • Narrow AI: Also called "Weak AI". Performance is limited to one specific domain.

Types Summary

  • Reactive Pure Present Response
  • Limited Memory Uses Recent Past
  • Theory of Mind Social Interaction
  • Self Awareness Self-Existential Goals
